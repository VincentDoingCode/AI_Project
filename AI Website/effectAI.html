About AI

What is AI?

AI is in short an machine or system that mimic human intelligence to perform task and can improve themself base on the information they collect.
Some examples of AI are

  • Self Driving Cars
  • Smart Assistant
  • Virtual Travel Assistant

Dangers of AI

So how is AI dangerous? Simply the pace of the field of AI is progressing extremely fast and people are worried if there are any precaution for the possibility of AI being uncontrollable. We all know the benefit and the capability of AI.

Potential Worries About AI

  • Autonomous Weapons
  • Job Automation
  • AI Bias
  • Invasion of privacy

There are many worries about AI and its potential danger. The list above are just some examples of potential dnager that AI will bring.
These problems have emerged ever since the world had seen the power of AI. These worries are not alone. Famous Physicist, Stephen Hawking told an audience in Lisbon, Portugal about how the development of AI could be the "worse event in the history of our civilization".
